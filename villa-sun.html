Villa Sun

Two bedroom villa for rent in Seminyak

About Villa Sun

Villa Sun is one funky nice villa all right, and perfectly priced and positioned to let the good times roll and to allow highest level of relaxation move in and take over your life. Even on rainy days and tropical nights the sun is always shining in its colourful decorated interiors with 26 miniature suns emblazoned in terracotta across the burnt-orange walls in a montage of happiness and smiles, while the round shape of the living room also representing our life-giving star.

Living Room

Entered through a wooden gate that leads into the garden and pool area, the living area is furnished with a large round (and sun-shaped) sofa placed in front of big-screen TV with two beanbags in gold and silver to add some more colour and fun to the seating arrangements. The designer dining table is stylish with solid wood legs covered by a circular piece of tempered glass, and itâ€™s attended by six fashionable bar chairs, which add to the already chic interiors of the villa.

Bedrooms

The two bedrooms have king-size beds and wardrobes, and sit opposite each other and lead off the living room. They are of similar size and are again designed with the dominant orange colour of the Sun. From bedroom one sliding doors lead to the 8 x 4 metres swimming pool while the doors on bedroom two open out into a well-manicured garden area with garden furniture offering a private chill out space or a venue for romantic dinners under the stars. Each bedroom has an en-suite bathroom with the same amenities but a slightly different layout. The poolside bedroom has only a rain shower while the garden-side bedroom has both rain shower and bathtub. Both enjoy the softest towels, bathrobes, and handmade ceramics and toiletries, which have been specially created for 4S Villas.

Kitchen

The villa kitchen is well equipped with a stove, microwave oven, blender, toaster and a collection of pots, pans and utensils ready to make a meal for any occasion.

Staff

Our smiling and attentive butlers will use the kitchen to cook your daily breakfast in the morning without any time limits for the last orders. The team at 4S villas working together to ensure that your stay is comfortable and relaxed. Don't hesitate to ask them to replace your stock of drinks or prepare tropical coctails and juices or even organise BBQ dinner right in your garden.

4S Villas are an oasis of serenity deep in the heart of vibrant Seminyak, an exciting international playground brimming with world class boutiques, spas, gourmet restaurants, bars, beach clubs and night life venues, and all within easy walking distance. Feel free to ask our villa staff for any recommendations on where to go, or to book a table on your behalf.

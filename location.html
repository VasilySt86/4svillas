    4S villas at seminyak square

4S Villas are an oasis of serenity deep in the heart of vibrant Seminyak, an exciting international playground brimming with world class boutiques, spas, gourmet restaurants, bars, beach clubs and night life venues, and all within easy walking distance.

The villas are just two-minutes’ walk from Seminyak Square, a shopping complex featuring a good range of cafes and shops, as well as a liquor store, while slightly further up the main road is Bali’s famous “Eat Street”, with loads of great bars and restaurants offering cuisines from around the world.

The beach is about seven-minute walk away and is home to such venues as Ku De Ta, La Lucciola, Potato Head and the W Hotel.

The beach offers sun, sand and surf, glorious coastline views, and a busy, buzzy atmosphere, especially in the late afternoons when visitors, expats and locals mingle and gather to watch yet another one of Bali’s legendary sunsets.

Heading slightly further afield into the uber-chic Petitenget district and no more than ten minutes by taxi and you will be among some of the most happening restaurants in Southeast Asia with the likes of Sarong, Metis, Mama san, Merah Putih and Charlie just waiting to wow you with their food, drink and good times.

Feel free to ask our villa staff for any recommendations on where to go, or to make bookings on your behalf.

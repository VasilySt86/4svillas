  • Sports and Beach Clubs

    Enjoy free access to the largest beach club on the island and the best on the island family sports club

We have provided everything to offer our guests a complete range of services during their holiday at 4S villas, including sports and beach clubs!

Rates inclusive temporary membership of the island's best family sports club - Finn's Recreational Club: a well-equipped gym, a 25-meter lap pool and children's pools, water slides, tennis and squash courts (tennis must be booked in advance, the availability of slots can not be guaranteed, especially in the High and Peak Season). In addition, the club's infrastructure includes bowling and a super modern trampoline centre, two restaurants, a sports bar and a magnificent Spa-center, as well as group classes and personal training (for an additional fee with discounts for holders of temporary membership cards) *. You can enjoy 50% discount on Sunday's roast menu.

Visiting of special events is paid additionally (concerts, show programs, holidays), according to the schedule of the club.

For full schedule of paid and free events visit: http://finnsrecclub.com/whatson

Watch FINN'S Recreational Club video

But that's not all. Holders of temporary Finn's Club membership cards can visit the island's largest Finn's Beach Club without paying a minimum deposit to reserve a place under the sun on their large beds and sunbeds **. The beach club regularly hosts evening shows and parties, all evening activities are paid extra, according to the policies of Finn's clubs.

For full schedule of programs and parties visit: http://www.finnsbeachclub.com/whats-on/

Both clubs are located at a distance of a 15-minute taxi ride (guests pay taxi by themselves). For the convenience of the guests, a free shuttle is organized between the clubs.

Watch FINN'S Beach Club video

* 4S villas Platinum membership at the Finn's Recreational Club is limited to 4 people per day for all 4S villas guests. We recommend booking the cards in advance, and apologize if not all guests can take advantage of this complimentary service on one of the days of your holiday. Day passes are always available for purchase at both clubs.

** The cost of replacement of lost cards is IDR 500.000 per card.

*** Beach and club towels are provided by both clubs for a small surcharge. 4S villas managemenet reserve the right to collect the cost of lost beach towels during the check-out.
